1.4 NullRip is "done"!?
Yeah… this wasn’t supposed to be a whole thing.
But here we are.

NullRip is now a complete DVD ripping + preview tool.

Features:
- Scan discs using HandBrakeCLI
- View titles, chapters, audio, and subtitle tracks
- Select exactly what you want to rip
- Per-chapter selection + toggle all
- Live progress + ETA during ripping
- Auto-open output folder on completion
- ▶ Chapter Preview (VLC integration)
  Click a chapter > opens VLC > jumps directly to that chapter on the disc
  No temp files, no guessing, no hacks

Notes:
- VLC required for preview (apt or flatpak supported)
- Uses direct DVD access for accurate playback
